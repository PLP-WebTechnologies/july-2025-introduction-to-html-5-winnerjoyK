Homemade Margherita Pizza

Simple, fresh, and bursting with flavor the perfect homemade pizza recipe.

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (preferably homemade or San Marzano)
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into a thin round shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border.
  4. Arrange mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  5. Bake the pizza for 10–12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  6. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil leaves, drizzle olive oil, and serve.

Pro Tips

For a crispier crust, preheat your baking tray or pizza stone in the oven. Add chili flakes for a little kick
